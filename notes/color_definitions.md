Colors:

- red — uptempo, scary, more dissonant
- orange — major chords, inspiring
- yellow — pure joy, relaxing/chill
- green — nature sounds, chill/calm, yoga, elves, surface water
- blue — deep water theme (whales, subnautica), minor chords, sad, choir
- purple — mysterious, spiritual, maj7 chords
- brown — fantasy dwarfs, drums, horns, low-pitch
- black — noir, dark, space, alien sounds, unsettling, synthetic
- white — calm, celestial, snow, single frequency sound, choir, reverb


Scales:

- Red — Phrygian dominant scale, sawtooth oscillator, distortion + short reverb, 168 BPM at 16th notes. Random dissonant leaps (tritones, minor 2nds) for that unsettling feel.

- Orange — C major, triangle oscillator, chorus + reverb, 110 BPM at 8th notes. Arpeggiated patterns with stepwise motion, feels ascending and hopeful.

- Yellow — Pentatonic major, sine oscillator, heavy reverb, 80 BPM at quarter notes. Sparse pattern with null rests for breathing room — feels spacious and
  calm.
